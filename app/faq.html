1. What is the RTC Household Travel Survey? Back to List

The Regional Transportation Commission Household Travel Survey (RTC HHTS) is the first in-depth study of household travel behavior in Washoe County in 10 years. Beginning in June, the RTC will be asking approximately 2,600 households to identify where and how they traveled on a specific, designated travel day (24 hours). In order to ensure a representative sample for the study, each household will also be asked a series of detailed questions about the householdâ€™s socioeconomic characteristics. When completed, this data will be used to estimate how much travel is generated by all households across the County.
