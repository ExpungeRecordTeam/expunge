Based on the answers you gave and Texas law, you may qualify for an Order of Nondisclosure.

You will need to fill out one form and file it with the county. Download this Petition for an Order of Nondisclosure, fill it out using your Order of Deferred Adjudication as a reference and sign it. You do not need to use a notary.

This instruction form will help you fill out the petition correctly.

Go to Travis County District Clerk, located 1000 Guadalupe St, Austin, TX 78701. File your Petition along with a copy of your Order of Deferred Adjudication with the District Clerk. You will be charged a fee of $330.

If you cannot afford the fee, download this fee waiver form and file it with your petition.
