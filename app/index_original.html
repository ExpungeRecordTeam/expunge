Welcome!

The Washoe County Regional Transportation Commission (RTC) and your local area governments work to provide a safe, efficient transportation system that supports economic opportunity and livable communities for Washoe County.

To help best meet the transportation needs in your local area and throughout the state, we're asking for your help. If you and your household members can tell us about your daily transportation habits by participating in our Travel Survey, it will help us plan future transportation improvements that will best serve your needs.

Making sure that your transportation needs are met is a top priority for RTC. It's important that everyone's travel habits are included as we develop transportation solutions for the future:

  • Whether you travel a lot or a little.

  • Whether you travel by car, bus, or simply by biking or walking.
