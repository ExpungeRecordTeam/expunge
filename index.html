Start with a clean slate

You had a run-in with the criminal system. You may have a criminal record and it may be preventing you from bettering your life.

However, you can do something about it. We are part of Open Austin, a civic initiative to address issues in Austin. We created this site to make it easier for you to pursue expunction. To find out if you are eligible for expunction in Travis County Texas please use our Eligibility App or visit our page on the Expunction Process for more information.

What does it mean to have your criminal record expunged?

Expunction gives you a clean slate. Having criminal charges on your record can prevent you from finding employment, public housing, and other government services. Expunging your criminal record can make accessing work, housing, and services easier.
